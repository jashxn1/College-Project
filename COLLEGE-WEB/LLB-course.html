Programme Structure : (LLB )


LLB is a 3-year full time degree programme which includes 6 semesters with training (industrial) after 5th semester. The PG degree is awarded by PUSSGRC.
LLB is a three-year integrated LLB course that candidates are eligible to pursue after completing Graduation. The Law course is an amalgamation of Humanities and Law streams, which allows candidates to study subjects such as History, Sociology, Administrative Law, Criminology, Family Law and so on. This course is extremely popular option for students completing Senior Secondary Education. Many top colleges and universities offer this course and have a separate Department of Law. Lakhs of candidates take admission to this course every year, proving the growing demand and popularity of five-year integrated law programme Eligibility Criteria : Candidate of any stream (Science, Commerce or Arts) securing 50% or more (45 % marks only for SC/ST/OBC candidate) at graduation. Student must be required intermediate Knowledge of Computer operator.

  1. Reservation of seats is strictly according to the Government guidelines.
  2. Management Quota/NRI 25% seats have been reserved as the management quota.
  3. Admission to these seats will be made at the institute level based on merit.
